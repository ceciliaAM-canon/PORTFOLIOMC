Medical Laboratory Scientist

LEKMA Hospital, Teshie

As a Medical Laboratory Scientist at LEKMA Hospital, I developed a strong foundation in precision, accuracy, and data integrity through hands-on diagnostic work. I performed comprehensive manual and automated culturing of diverse biological samples including blood, urine, stool, HVS, and urethral specimens, ensuring accurate results for patient diagnosis and treatment.

My responsibilities included conducting precise biochemical tests following Clinical Laboratory Standardized Institute guidelines and executing detailed hematological procedures such as malaria parasite identification, sickle cell testing, complete blood counts, and erythrocyte sedimentation rates. I gained specialized expertise in utilizing advanced GeneXpert PCR technology for accurate MTB diagnosis and rifampicin resistance detection, contributing to effective tuberculosis management.

Additionally, I managed critical blood banking procedures including ABO and Rh blood grouping and cross-matching for safe transfusions. This experience instilled in me the importance of meticulous data recording, quality control, and the critical role that reliable laboratory data plays in healthcare decision-making and patient outcomes.
